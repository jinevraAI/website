Why is jinevra special?

Jinevra democratizes access to powerful natural language processing tools to fill gaps in exsiting tools and to tackle some persistent challenges in language classification and bias detection in English.

Why this, why now?

Jinevra was born after Eleanor Stribling ran a number of side projects in language analysis, presented results at a number of conferences (PyCon and US, DjangoCon, Codeland) and got many questions about how more people could use these tools or get involved with building them.
